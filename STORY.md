# STORY.md — Eggbert (Loose Draft)

## Tone (locked)

**EarthBound-weird**: Everyday mundane with surreal undercurrent. NPCs talk about normal life (mopping floors, paying bills, losing shoes) but odd things are taken for granted — humming fridges, floating mailboxes, drain monsters that nobody will confirm or deny. The world is sincerely weird, not ironically weird.

## Protagonist

**Eggbert (EB)** — a kid in an egg-costume that nobody comments on. This is completely normal. Married to Eggatha, has a child (EJ). Works at a factory.

---

## Story Beats (rough sequence, not final)

### 1. Factory (tutorial-lite)
EB finishes work at the factory. The Jamitor (janitor + jam) teaches basic skills. Officer Bacon arrests EB for being in the wrong place at the wrong time. Gets shipped to Eggs Isle prison island.

### 2. Eggs Isle Intake
Shared cell with Frank, a cellmate who becomes a friend. Frank explains the prison layout. Meet other inmates — some helpful, some hostile, some just weird. Learn about the prison routine.

### 3. Kitchen
Kitchen duty. Grandpa Smith encounter about the value of ~~mercy~~ patience. Chef NPC drops hints about the Sunnysides. KP mini-game (puzzle: serve slop requests or timing/whack-a-mole). Oatmeal encounter.

### 4. Courtyard
Egguardo has info/keys to the warden's quarters but wants something in return (quiz, deal, favor). Some egg NPCs start dropping references to "Sunnysides."

### 5. Warden's Quarters
Sneak into the warden's room. Find guard rotation schedule, prison map, Officer Bacon backstory. Yogurt encounter.

### 6. Rec Room
Waffles is guarding a hole in the wall covered by a poster. Can fight or befriend him. Mercy route: if you spare him, he opens up about getting pigeonholed as the tough guy.

### 7. Secret Tunnels
Maze under the prison. Sunnyside lore scattered around — diagrams, summoning circles. Cereal encounter.

### 8. Sunnyside Shrine
Revelation: the Sunnysides are a cult of eggs who believe breakfast is the One True Meal and want to resurrect the Great Toast God to obliterate all non-breakfast foods. The goopy mini-bosses (Oatmeal, Yogurt, Cereal) were failed summonings. EB gets caught and thrown in solitary.

### 9. Solitary
Low point. Sensory deprivation cell ("egg carton for one"). Hidden message / realization.

### 10. Beach (final encounter)
Sunnyside Leader confrontation — whether EB wins or loses, the Great Toast God gets summoned. GTG doesn't care about the cult. Sees EB's pure soul and sends him home.

### Endings
- **Good Egg**: Returns home, family relieved. "Still the same egg I love."
- **Mid Egg**: Something's off. Family is meh or weird.
- **Bad Egg**: Prison changed EB. Kid doesn't recognize him. "You look so hard boiled now."

Tracked via WorldFlags: which bosses were spared vs defeated determines ending tier.
